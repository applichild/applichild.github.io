Get the best 3D animal game and animations app in the world with animals in the farm, jungle, pole, and ocean. We have also specified a section for various birds.
Zookidi is an interactive and very attractive 3D game that entertains kids and helps them learn more about the names, actions, and sounds of animals. This 3D game includes 100 animals in total.

This animated zoo tour can easily entertain toddlers with easy navigation. It offers high Definition animal animations, and by a simple touch on animals, it produces animal sounds.

Animals' names come in 5 languages (English, French, Spanish, Chinese, and Farsi). This feature will help kids to learn the dictation and pronunciation of the animals' names in multi-languages.

The graphical quality of animations will be adjusted automatically to the specifications of your device and it can also be changed manually.
